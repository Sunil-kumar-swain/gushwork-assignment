Frequently Asked Questions

What is the purpose of HDPE pipes for water supply?

HDPE pipes are designed for safe and efficient water supply. Non-toxic, corrosion-resistant, and smooth-bore, they are ideal for potable water delivery across residential, agricultural, and industrial pipelines.

What are the benefits of using HDPE over metal pipes?

HDPE is lightweight (1/7th the weight of GI pipe), corrosion-proof, flexible, and has a 50+ year service life with virtually zero maintenance. It dramatically reduces total installation and lifecycle costs.

How is HDPE pipe manufactured?

Through a precision extrusion process: PE100 grade resin is melted, forced through an annular die, then cooled and vacuum-sized for exact diameter control. Every batch is quality-tested to IS 4984 standards.

What are the common applications of HDPE piping?

Agriculture irrigation, potable water distribution, gas distribution networks, sewage and drainage systems, mining slurry transport, industrial chemical lines, and telecommunications ducting.

Can HDPE pipes be customized?

Yes. We offer custom diameters (20mm–1600mm), wall thicknesses (SDR 11–SDR 33), pressure ratings (PN 2.5–PN 16), and coil lengths. Contact our technical team for bespoke solutions.

Advanced HDPE Pipe Manufacturing Process

State-of-the-art extrusion technology ensuring consistent quality and dimensional accuracy in every pipe we manufacture.

High-Grade Raw Material Selection

Only PE100 grade virgin resin with optimal molecular weight distribution is used. Every batch is tested for melt flow index and density before entering the production line.

  • PE100 grade material
  • Optimal molecular weight distribution

Precision Extrusion Process

Advanced single-screw extruders melt and homogenize the PE compound, pushing it through an annular die to form a continuous pipe profile with uniform wall thickness.

  • Uniform wall thickness control
  • Temperature-regulated barrel zones

Controlled Cooling System

Multi-stage vacuum and spray cooling systems rapidly reduce pipe temperature while maintaining dimensional stability and optimal crystalline structure.

  • Vacuum spray cooling technology
  • Optimal crystallization patterns

Vacuum Sizing Calibration

Vacuum sizing tanks ensure precise outer diameter while internal pressure maintains perfect roundness and wall thickness uniformity across every meter of pipe.

  • Precision diameter control
  • Consistent wall thickness

Rigorous Quality Control

Comprehensive testing at every stage including hydrostatic pressure tests, dimensional checks, and material property verification per IS 4984 standards.

  • Hydrostatic pressure testing
  • Dimensional accuracy verification

Automated Pipe Marking

Laser and inkjet marking systems print essential information including size, pressure rating, material grade, manufacturing date, and BIS certification marks.

  • Permanent laser marking
  • BIS certification imprint

Precision Cutting & Coiling

Automated cutting systems ensure clean, burr-free ends at precise lengths. Smaller diameter pipes are coiled using controlled winding systems for easy transport.

  • Automated length control
  • Burr-free cutting technology

Secure Packaging & Dispatch
